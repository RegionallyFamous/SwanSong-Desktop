# Code of conduct

SwanSong is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience, identity, background, or ability.

Be considerate, specific, and constructive. Critique work rather than people;
assume good intent while taking impact seriously; respect privacy; and do not
publish another person's private information or copyrighted game material.
Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, sustained
disruption, and retaliation are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior is unsafe or persistently disruptive.
Enforcement decisions should protect affected people, minimize unnecessary
disclosure, and be explained to the person receiving them when practical.

Report routine moderation concerns to the maintainers on the relevant GitHub
thread. For a confidential report, use the repository's private **Report a
vulnerability** form and begin the report title with `Conduct:`. Do not include
private game files or unrelated personal information.
