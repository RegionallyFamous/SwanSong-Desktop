The workshop just got bigger.

SwanSong 0.5.0 adds a native writing desk and a guarded bridge to real WonderSwan cartridges.

  • Write from idea to edition. Story Forge keeps outline, draft, revision, art, continuity, rights, EPUB, and PDF work together.
  • Continue into a game. Hand a revision-ready novel to SwanSong Studio for a WonderSwan adaptation.
  • Back up real cartridges. Cartridge Lab makes verified ROM and save backups through a WonderSwan Color or SwanCrystal.
  • Restore saves carefully. Exact sizing, two-screen confirmation, physical A+B approval, and complete readback guard every write.
  • Enjoy a clearer SwanSong. The README, setup guides, FAQ, Support, and Wiki now lead with what you can do.

Story Forge and Cartridge Lab require separately obtained local projects or authorized hardware. SwanSong includes no games or original system firmware. Read the full release notes.
